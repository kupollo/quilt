Workshop Description

This lovely twin sized quilt (70" x 81") you CAN make! During all process I will be with you. Every week we will be together online where I will demonstrate each stage of creating this quilt and where you can ask me any question and concern you may have.

Pattern along with all the fabric measurements needed for your quilt top, binding and backing you will get by email.

Let's do it together!

Recommended Materials

What tools are required to have:

  • Sewing machine
  • You may have fabric of your choice by the first class, or you will have one week after our first lesson to choose. It's up to you. In our second class we start working with fabrics.

Note: All lessons will be recorded, so if you cannot start with us, you can always make a break and continue when you are ready.

What tools and notions may be helpful:

  • Rotary cutter
  • Quilting rulers
  • Cutting mat
  • 50wt Cotton Thread
